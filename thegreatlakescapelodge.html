Make the Great Lakescape Lodge your destination in beautiful Door County. Nestled on the shores of lake Michigan at Balley's harbor, our lodge is close to everything that Door County has offer.

We are a family-owned and operated establishment and have beed part of the Northern Wisconsin vacation scene for 25 years. The logde is great place for a romantic getaway or a family reunion. We also cater weddings and can provide all of the amenities to make your time here a special one.

It's our goal to make every guest feel pampered; from the gourment breakfast and afternoon tea, to the special touches you will find in every room. Families can enjoy our rec room and pool and outdoor equipment for boating and cycling; but there are alos quite secluded spots for people who are jsut llong ro get away for a restful weekend.

Come and see for yourself what keeps our guest coming back year after year. We are open April 1st to October 31st. If you have never visited Door County before, let us be your guide to the wonderful opportunities that await you.
